How we met

We both attended Hope College and unknowingly crossed paths many times during our four years. However, it took a trip halfway across the world to finally meet. Our initial meeting took place on the flight to Australia where we both spent a month studying abroad. After an amazing trip down-under we were both intrigued.We went our separate ways for the summer, but sure enough, just a few days before school started we literally "ran" into each other on 15th street in Holland (where we both lived) and were soon reminded of the chemistry. A few months later we fell in love.

How we got engaged

Andy presented me with tickets to see the Russian ballet Swan Lake for my Christmas present. After spending a wonderful afternoon with friends from school who now live in Chicago, Andy surprised me with a delicious dinner at a gourmet vegan restaurant followed by the ballet at the Auditorium theatre. Little did I know he had one more surprise in store... he took me to a rooftop lounge over-looking the city where we were treated with VIP service, a bottle of champagne and a reserved spot next to a cozy fireplace. Next thing I knew he was down on one knee asking me to be his wife! On our way home we got to see both of our families to celebrate. It was truly a perfect weekend.
